Automation Solutions That Deliver Results

We engineer robotic systems for every need in your production line.

Robotic Palletizing

Hexon's palletizing systems handle high-speed stacking, layer formation, and mixed-SKU palletizing with consistent accuracy. Designed for food, pharma, and FMCG lines, these systems integrate with existing conveyors and ensure stable, damage-free stacking.

  • Throughput up to 1,000 cycles per hour
  • Supports standard and mixed pallet patterns
  • Vision-assisted layer verification to reduce stacking errors

Robotic Welding

Our robotic welding cells deliver consistent MIG, TIG, arc, and spot welding with reduced operator dependency. Systems are configured with seam tracking and adaptive tuning for real-world part variation.

  • Repeatability of ±0.10 mm (robot dependent)
  • Seam tracking using touch sensing or vision
  • Consistent weld quality with reduced rework
Get in touch →

Robotic Painting

Hexon's robotic painting systems ensure controlled spray application, uniform coating, and reduced manual exposure. Suitable for automotive components, fabricated parts, and appliances.

  • Upto 30% reduction in paint consumption vs manual processes
  • Uniform coating thickness across batches
  • Safety-compliant system design for hazardous environments
Get in touch →

Robotic Inspection

Vision-guided robotic inspection systems perform in-line quality checks including dimensional validation and surface inspection, without interrupting production flow.

  • Detects defects down to 0.2 mm
  • Real-time pass/fail sorting with data logging
  • Integrates with existing conveyor lines

Pick & Place

High-speed delta and SCARA robots handle pick-and-place and light assembly tasks across FMCG, pharma, and electronics. Designed for reliability in continuous operations.

  • Up to 120 picks per minute
  • Gentle handling with vacuum or servo grippers
  • Tooling changeover within 15 minutes

Machine Tending

Robotic machine tending systems automate loading and unloading of CNCs and presses, improving machine utilization and reducing manual intervention.

  • 25% improvement in typical machine utilization
  • Enables extended unattended operation
  • Integrated safety systems (area scanners, guarding)
